In 1969, Wes Anderson was born in Houston Texas to a Ann and Melver Anderson.
He attended the Univeristy Of Texas, Austin where obtained his film degree
and befriended famous actor, Owen Wilson. His debut film, BottleRocket,
was the beginning of his impactful and groundbreaking film career. His numerous films
have generated several Academy and BAFTA awards, with his 2014,
The Grand Budapest Hotel, winnning the majority of them. His dynamic, clever,
and bold film style make him one of the most interesting filmmakers of all time.
