“Failure is a badge of honour; it means you risked failure. If you don’t risk failure you’re never
going to do anything that’s different than what you’ve already done, or what somebody else has done.” - Charlie Kaufman

Charlie Kaufman is known for his cerebral and thought provoking films. Every time someone leaves
a film of his they are forced to take a deeper reflection on themselves and their lives. His writing
style and complex make his films all the more interesting, but also makes his movies geared
towards a certain audience as well. His brilliant mind attempts to combine the human mind
and experience into a 90-120 minute piece and he does a pretty good job at it.
