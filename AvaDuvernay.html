“All the traditional models for doing things are collapsing; from music to publishing to film, and it's a wide
open door for people who are creative to do what they need to do without having institutions block their art.” - Ava DeVernay

Ava DuVernay is an up and coming film director who is making a new for herself by directing
groundbreaking pieces such as 13th, and Selma. Her work resonates across color lines.
Her brilliance and excellence can be scene in each and every movie she directs. She was the first black
women to direct a movie with an over 100 million dollar budget. Her work and pioneering is making
ways for young women all over who desire to follow in her footsteps. Her movies send messages and so does her life.
